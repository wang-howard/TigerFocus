Our Mission

Our mission is to provide Princeton students with a highly effective and intuitive Pomodoro and task management app that helps them optimize their productivity and achieve their academic goals. We strive to create a tool that simplifies the process of managing tasks, enhances focus, and encourages a healthy work-life balance. Our app is designed to empower students with the tools they need to stay organized, manage their time efficiently, and ultimately succeed in their academic pursuits.
